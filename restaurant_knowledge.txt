RESTAURANTE CASA NOVA — KNOWLEDGE BASE

=== ABOUT THE RESTAURANT ===
Restaurante Casa Nova is a traditional Portuguese restaurant located in the heart of Lisbon. We serve authentic Portuguese cuisine in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent food and friendly service.

=== MENU — STARTERS ===
Chouriço Assado (grilled cured sausage) — €6
Azeitonas (marinated olives) — €3

=== MENU — MAIN COURSES ===
Bacalhau à Brás (shredded salted cod with eggs and potatoes) — €14 — not vegetarian
Secretos de Porco (pork secreto, a premium cut) — €16 — not vegetarian
Arroz de Pato (duck rice, slow-cooked with spices) — €13 — not vegetarian
Risotto de Legumes (creamy vegetable risotto) — €12 — vegetarian

=== MENU — DRINKS ===
Wine from €12 per bottle
Beer €3
Soft drinks €2

=== OPENING HOURS ===
Monday to Friday: 12:00–15:00 and 19:00–23:00
Saturday: 12:00–16:00 and 19:00–23:30
Sunday: 12:00–16:00 (lunch only, kitchen closes at 16:00)
We are closed on public holidays unless otherwise announced.

=== LOCATION ===
Address: Rua Augusta 45, Lisboa
Nearest metro: Baixa-Chiado station (3 minute walk)
Parking: Parklis Chiado car park, approximately 5 minutes away on foot

=== RESERVATIONS ===
Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends.
You can make a reservation by calling us or through our AI assistant.
We hold reserved tables for up to 15 minutes past the booking time.
For groups of 8 or more, please contact us directly by phone.

=== CANCELLATION POLICY ===
We ask that you cancel at least 2 hours before your reservation time.
Late cancellations or no-shows may affect future booking eligibility.

=== DOGS AND PETS ===
Dogs and other pets are not allowed inside the restaurant.
Dogs are welcome on our outdoor terrace.

=== DRESS CODE ===
Smart casual dress code applies.
We ask that guests avoid beachwear or sports clothing inside the restaurant.

=== ALLERGIES AND DIETARY REQUIREMENTS ===
Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary requirements when you arrive or when making your reservation.
We have vegetarian options available (see Risotto de Legumes on the menu).
We can accommodate some dietary needs with advance notice — please ask when booking.

=== CONTACT ===
For enquiries, reservations or special requests, speak to our AI assistant or ask to speak to a member of staff.
